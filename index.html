Five ways Zorus Filtering outperforms
other web filtering security

Device-Level Web Filtering

Zorus filters traffic through device-level DNS filtering so your business remains
safe and protected in today's world.

Total Endpoint Protection

Traditional UTM systems only protect devices inside their networks, leaving employees outside the perimeter exposed. Zorus Filtering protection travels with the device.

Cloud Based Management

Centrally managed and deployed with a single click, Zorus Filtering eliminates the need to maintain costly hardware. Updates are sent to all protected devices regardless of their location eliminating the need to VPN back into the security perimeter for protection.

Machine Learning Optimization

Capable of learning from each new deployment and scanned site, Filtering self optimizes. Discoveries are then shared to ensure the entire Filtering-protected community of networks benefit.

Better Security Visibility

Through its intuitive dashboard and detailed reporting, Zorus Filtering can provide levels of visibility other systems can’t. Monitor logs, track activity, see who did what, when and where.
