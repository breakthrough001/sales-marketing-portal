Tutorials

How to use:

Go to the MSP's website and right click on their logo and click on 'Save Image'.

Next go to Filtering or Engagement and upload their logo.

You can add their phone number and email from their website. If they only have an email or only have a phone number that is ok too. Just paste it in the input field.

Best practice to make the phone number in the (XXX) XXX-XXXX for legibility.

If you have an MSP's logo that does not have a transparent background then use this website Remove Background.
